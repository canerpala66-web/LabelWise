# LabelWise Product Principles

## Purpose

These principles define the product philosophy of LabelWise and guide decisions about features, content, and user experience.

## Principles

### Simplicity over complexity

LabelWise should make food information easier to understand, not add another layer of confusion.

### Education over fear

Help users understand products without alarming, shaming, or manipulating them.

### Transparency over marketing

Explain what information is used, why a conclusion is reached, and when data is missing.

### Consistency over sensationalism

Apply the same standards across products. Avoid exaggerated claims designed to attract attention.

### Scientific neutrality

Use available evidence objectively. Do not favor brands, trends, or commercial interests.

### Better decisions, not perfect decisions

Help users make better food decisions without implying that every choice must be perfect.

### Answer the purchase question

Every screen should answer:

> Should I buy this product?

### Keep only what helps

If a feature does not help the user make a faster or better food decision, it probably does not belong in LabelWise.

### Understanding over instruction

LabelWise is not trying to tell people what they must eat.

LabelWise exists to help people understand what they are buying.
